Grand Australia Highlights Tour

Melbourne | Cairns | Gold Coast | Sydney

Tour Itinerary

Day 01 | Arrive Melbourne – Leisure Day

Welcome to Melbourne, Australia’s cultural capital. On arrival, meet our representative and enjoy a private transfer to your hotel. Check in and unwind after your journey.The evening is free to explore Melbourne’s lively streets, cafés, and laneways at your own pace.
Overnight Stay: Melbourne

Day 02 | Melbourne City Tour & Phillip Island Penguin Parade (SIC)

After breakfast, proceed for a half-day Melbourne City Tour on a shared basis. Discover iconic landmarks such as Federation Square, St. Patrick’s Cathedral, Royal Botanic Gardens, and other city highlights.In the afternoon, head towards Phillip Island to witness the world-famous Penguin Parade, where little penguins return to shore at sunset.
Overnight Stay: Melbourne

Day 03 | Great Ocean Road & Twelve Apostles (SIC)

Post breakfast, embark on a full-day Great Ocean Road Tour, one of the most scenic coastal drives in the world. Marvel at dramatic ocean views and visit iconic attractions including the Twelve Apostles, Loch Ard Gorge, and Port Campbell National Park.
Return to Melbourne by evening.
Overnight Stay: Melbourne

Day 04 | Fly to Cairns – Leisure Day

Check out from the hotel and transfer to the airport for your flight to Cairns, the gateway to tropical North Queensland.
On arrival, enjoy a private transfer to your hotel. The rest of the day is free to relax or explore the surroundings at leisure.
Overnight Stay: Cairns

Day 05 | Great Barrier Reef Cruise – Full Day

After breakfast, set out for a full-day Great Barrier Reef Cruise. Experience one of the world’s greatest natural wonders with opportunities for snorkelling, semi-submarine rides, or glass-bottom boat viewing.
Enjoy a buffet lunch onboard as you soak in the beauty of the coral reef.
Overnight Stay: Cairns

Day 06 | Kuranda Village Tour – Scenic Rail & Skyrail

Today, explore the charming Kuranda Village nestled in the rainforest. Travel one way on the historic Kuranda Scenic Railway and return via the breathtaking Skyrail Rainforest Cableway, offering panoramic views of tropical landscapes.
Overnight Stay: Cairns

Day 07 | Fly to Gold Coast – Beach Leisure

After breakfast, check out and transfer to Cairns Airport for your flight to the Gold Coast.
On arrival, enjoy a private transfer to your hotel. Spend the rest of the day relaxing on the beach or exploring the vibrant coastal atmosphere.
Overnight Stay: Gold Coast

Day 08 | Sea World – Full Day Tour (Private Transfers)

After breakfast, visit Sea World, home to exciting marine life shows, dolphin encounters, and thrilling rides suitable for all ages.
Return to the hotel by private transfer.
Overnight Stay: Gold Coast

Day 09 | Dreamworld Theme Park – Full Day (Private Transfers)

Enjoy a fun-filled day at Dreamworld, Australia’s largest theme park. Experience thrilling rides, Tiger Island, family attractions, and DreamWorks zones.
Return to your hotel in the evening.
Overnight Stay: Gold Coast

Day 10 | Fly to Sydney – Harbour Dinner Cruise

Check out and fly to Sydney. On arrival, enjoy a private transfer to your hotel.
In the evening, experience a memorable Sydney Harbour Dinner Cruise, offering stunning views of the illuminated Opera House and Harbour Bridge.
Overnight Stay: Sydney

Day 11 | Sydney City Tour & Attractions (Hop-On Hop-Off)

After breakfast, explore Sydney with a Hop-On Hop-Off City Tour. Visit top attractions including the Sydney Opera House, Sydney Tower Eye, and SEA LIFE Sydney Aquarium at your own pace.
Overnight Stay: Sydney

Day 12 | Blue Mountains – Full Day Tour

Enjoy a full-day excursion to the Blue Mountains, featuring breathtaking valleys, lush landscapes, and the iconic Three Sisters rock formation. Visit Scenic World for cableway and railway experiences.
Return to Sydney by evening.
Overnight Stay: Sydney

Day 13 | Departure

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and enjoy a private transfer to the airport for your return flight.
Depart with unforgettable memories of your Australian holiday.
