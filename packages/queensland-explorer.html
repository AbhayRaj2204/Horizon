Ready to Plan Your Dream Journey?

Queensland Explorer – Brisbane, Sunshine Coast & Noosa

Self-Drive | Nature | Wildlife | Adventure

Tour Itinerary

Day 01 | Arrive in Brisbane – Self-Drive & City Exploration

Welcome to Brisbane, Queensland’s vibrant riverfront capital, celebrated for its sunny lifestyle, outdoor experiences, and relaxed urban charm.
On arrival at Brisbane Airport, collect your self-drive vehicle (Toyota Yaris or similar) and proceed to your hotel.Check-in as per standard hotel time (early check-in subject to availability).
Spend the afternoon and evening exploring Brisbane at your own pace. Wander through the lively CBD, admire heritage sandstone architecture, enjoy riverside walks, or soak up the city’s buzzing café and dining scene.
Optional Highlight – Story Bridge Adventure Climb
Take your adventure to new heights to new heights with a day or night climb on Brisbane’s iconic Story Bridge. After a safety briefing, follow your expert guide to the summit and enjoy breathtaking 360° views of the Brisbane River, Moreton Bay, Glass House Mountains, Mt. Coot-tha, and Lamington National Park.
Includes one complimentary group photo
Duration: Approx. 2 hours
Overnight stay in Brisbane.

Day 02 | Australia Zoo – The Ultimate Wildlife Experience

After breakfast, drive north to the Sunshine Coast for an unforgettable day at the world-famous Australia Zoo.
Founded by the legendary Steve Irwin, Australia Zoo offers one of the most authentic and interactive wildlife experiences in the world. Spread across over 110 acres and home to 1,200+ animals, this conservation-focused zoo brings you face-to-face with Australia’s unique fauna.
Highlights include:
• Cuddling a koala
• Feeding kangaroos
• Walking with wombats
• African Savannah experience
• Majestic tigers & red pandas
• Wildlife Warriors Show at the iconic Crocoseum featuring saltwater crocodiles, birds, and snakes
• Explore Bindi’s Island with treehouses, lemurs, tortoises, parrots & more
Return to your hotel after an action-packed day.
Overnight stay on the Sunshine Coast / Noosa region.

Day 03 | Maleny Botanical Gardens & Bird World

Enjoy breakfast before heading into the tranquil Queensland Hinterland, away from the coastal crowds.
Spend a relaxed day at Maleny Botanic Gardens & Bird World, surrounded by lush gardens, panoramic Glass House Mountain views, and vibrant birdlife. Wander through beautifully landscaped gardens, meet colourful parrots and exotic birds, and enjoy an open-air picnic from the café.
A perfect day for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts.
Drive back to the hotel.
Overnight stay in Noosa.

Day 04 | Great Beach Drive – 4WD Coastal Adventure

After breakfast, set off on the spectacular Great Beach Drive 4WD Tour.
Experience the raw beauty of the Sunshine Coast as you travel along endless sandy beaches, dramatic dunes, rainforest tracks, and coastal lagoons. With expert guidance, explore highlights such as:
• 40-Mile Beach
• Red Canyon
• Coloured Sands
• Swim in a pristine saltwater lagoon
• Spot dolphins, sea turtles, and stingrays along the coast
A thrilling off-road adventure combined with stunning natural scenery.
Return to the hotel.
Overnight stay in Noosa.

Day 05 | Noosa Everglades Eco Safari – Day Cruise

After breakfast, depart for the Noosa Everglades Eco Safari, one of Australia’s most pristine and breathtaking ecosystems.
Board a purpose-built eco vessel from Habitat Noosa and cruise into Australia’s only Everglades, often referred to as the “River of Mirrors”.
Highlights include:
• Visit Fig Tree Point, an important Indigenous cultural site
• Cruise through ancient mirrored waterways and subtropical rainforest
• Morning tea at historic Harry’s Hut
• Expert commentary on flora, fauna, and regional history
• Home to 44% of Australia’s native bird species
A peaceful, scenic, and truly unforgettable experience.
Return to the hotel.
Overnight stay in Noosa.

Day 06 | Depart Brisbane – Journey Home

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and drive to Brisbane Airport at the appropriate time for your onward flight.Return your self-drive vehicle at the airport.
Depart with wonderful memories of Queensland’s wildlife, beaches, and natural wonders—experiences you’ll cherish for a lifetime.
