10 Nights / 11 Days – New Zealand SIC Experience

Package 6

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Auckland | Sky Tower (SIC Transfer)

Welcome to Auckland, the “City of Sails”. On arrival, enjoy a SIC transfer to your hotel. After check-in, the remainder of the day is free to relax or explore at your own pace.
In the evening, visit the iconic Sky Tower, the tallest freestanding structure in the Southern Hemisphere. Enjoy spectacular views across the city and harbours, along with a wide range of dining and entertainment options.

Day 2: Auckland | City Highlights Tour

After breakfast, set out on a guided Auckland City Tour, showcasing the city’s volcanic landscape, coastal views and historic neighbourhoods.
The tour includes visits to scenic viewpoints overlooking the Waitematā Harbour and the charming seaside village of Devonport on Auckland’s North Shore. Travel in a small group with expert commentary from award-winning local guides, offering insight into Auckland’s culture, geography and history.

Day 3: Auckland – Rotorua via Waitomo Glowworm Caves

Following breakfast, check out and depart Auckland on a full-day journey to Rotorua via Waitomo. Travel through the fertile Waikato and King Country regions, with informative commentary from your local driver-guide along the way.
At Waitomo, enjoy a guided tour of the famous Glowworm Caves, formed over 30 million years ago. Walk through impressive limestone caverns before boarding a silent boat ride through the Glowworm Grotto, illuminated by thousands of glowworms.
Continue on to Rotorua, travelling through the picturesque Fitzgerald Glade, where native trees form a natural tunnel over the road.

Day 4: Rotorua | Te Puia, Agrodome & Rainbow Springs

After breakfast, join a half-day Rotorua sights tour. Begin at Te Puia, a centre of Māori culture and geothermal activity, featuring geysers, boiling mud pools and steaming vents.
Continue to Rainbow Springs Nature Park, home to native flora and fauna, including the iconic New Zealand kiwi. The tour concludes at the Agrodome, where you’ll enjoy a lively sheep shearing show and sheep dog demonstrations showcasing New Zealand’s farming heritage.

Day 5: Rotorua – Queenstown | Leisure Day

After breakfast, check out and take a SIC transfer to Rotorua Airport for your flight to Queenstown.
On arrival, enjoy a SIC transfer to your hotel. After check-in, the remainder of the day is free at leisure.
Queenstown is known for its vibrant dining and entertainment scene. A list of recommended cafés, restaurants and bars will be shared closer to your arrival. Optional activities and tours can be added on request.

Day 6: Queenstown | Milford Sound Cruise (Ex Queenstown)

After breakfast, depart on a full-day tour to Milford Sound, one of New Zealand’s most iconic natural attractions.
Board a scenic cruise through the fiord, taking in towering cliffs, deep blue waters and spectacular waterfalls including Bowen Falls and Stirling Falls. Keep an eye out for wildlife such as fur seals, penguins, dolphins and, occasionally, whales. Some vessels also feature underwater viewing areas.

Day 7: Queenstown | Skyline Gondola (Self Transfer)

After breakfast, make your own way to the Skyline Queenstown Gondola. Enjoy a scenic ride on the Southern Hemisphere’s steepest gondola, ascending 450 vertical metres to Bob’s Peak for panoramic views over Queenstown, Lake Wakatipu and the surrounding mountains.

Day 8: Queenstown – Franz Josef | Leisure Day

Following breakfast, check out and travel by SIC transfer to Franz Josef.
After arrival and hotel check-in, the remainder of the day is at leisure to explore the township or relax amidst the surrounding alpine and rainforest scenery.

Day 9: Franz Josef | Glacier Hot Pools

After breakfast, unwind at the Glacier Hot Pools, set within native rainforest. Enjoy access to the public hot pools, offering a peaceful and rejuvenating experience surrounded by natural bush and birdlife.
This tranquil retreat is an ideal way to relax after travelling along the West Coast or exploring the region.

Day 10: Franz Josef – Christchurch | TranzAlpine Train

After breakfast, check out and transfer to Greymouth Railway Station. Board the renowned TranzAlpine Train for a scenic journey to Christchurch, widely regarded as one of the world’s great train journeys.
Travel through dramatic alpine landscapes, river valleys and mountain passes. On arrival in Christchurch, transfer to your hotel and enjoy the evening at leisure.

Day 11: Departure | Christchurch

As per your flight schedule, enjoy a SIC transfer to Christchurch Airport for your onward journey, departing New Zealand with lasting memories of a truly memorable experience.
