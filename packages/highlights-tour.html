Australia Highlights Tour

Melbourne | Gold Coast | Sydney

Tour Itinerary

Day 01 | Arrive Melbourne – Yarra River Cruise

Welcome to Melbourne, Australia’s cultural capital. Upon arrival, meet our representative and enjoy a private transfer to your hotel. Check in and relax after your journey.
In the evening, experience the charm of the city with a Yarra River Cruise, offering beautiful views of Melbourne’s skyline and riverside attractions (at your own pace).
Overnight stay in Melbourne.

Day 02 | Melbourne City Tour & Phillip Island Penguin Parade (SIC)

After breakfast, set out on a half-day Melbourne City Tour on a shared basis. Discover iconic landmarks such as Federation Square, St. Patrick’s Cathedral, Fitzroy Gardens, Royal Botanic Gardens, and more.
Later in the day, travel to Phillip Island to witness the world-famous Penguin Parade, where little penguins emerge from the ocean at sunset—an unforgettable wildlife experience.
Overnight stay in Melbourne.

Day 03 | Great Ocean Road & 12 Apostles Tour (SIC)

Post breakfast, enjoy a full-day excursion along the spectacular Great Ocean Road, one of the most scenic coastal drives in the world.
Marvel at natural wonders including the 12 Apostles, Loch Ard Gorge, and the dramatic cliffs of Port Campbell National Park. Capture breathtaking ocean views before returning to Melbourne in the evening.
Overnight stay in Melbourne.

Day 04 | Fly to Gold Coast – Glow Worms Cave Experience

After breakfast, check out and transfer to the airport for your flight to the Gold Coast. Upon arrival, enjoy a private transfer to your hotel.
In the evening, experience the magical Glow Worms Cave, where thousands of glowing worms illuminate the ancient caves—a truly unique natural phenomenon.
Overnight stay in Gold Coast.

Day 05 | Sea World Theme Park (Private Transfers)

After breakfast, enjoy a full-day visit to Sea World, one of Australia’s most popular marine theme parks.
Enjoy exciting rides, captivating marine animal shows, dolphin encounters, and family-friendly attractions. Return to your hotel by private transfer.
Overnight stay in Gold Coast.

Day 06 | Dreamworld Theme Park (Private Transfers)

Today, head out for a fun-filled day at Dreamworld, Australia’s largest theme park.
Explore thrilling rides, Tiger Island, DreamWorks Experience zones, and attractions suitable for all age groups. After an exciting day, return to your hotel by private transfer.
Overnight stay in Gold Coast.

Day 07 | Fly to Sydney – Sydney Harbour Dinner Cruise

Check out from your hotel and fly to Sydney. Upon arrival, enjoy a private transfer to your hotel.
In the evening, indulge in a Sydney Harbour Dinner Cruise, featuring delicious cuisine and stunning night views of the Sydney Opera House and Harbour Bridge.
Overnight stay in Sydney.

Day 08 | Sydney City Tour & Attractions (Hop-On Hop-Off)

After breakfast, explore Sydney with a Big Bus Hop-On Hop-Off City Tour, offering flexibility to discover the city at your own pace.
Visit major attractions including the Sydney Opera House, Sydney Tower Eye, and SEA LIFE Sydney Aquarium.
Overnight stay in Sydney.

Day 09 | Blue Mountains Full-Day Tour

Today, enjoy a scenic excursion to the Blue Mountains, a UNESCO World Heritage region.
Visit the famous Three Sisters, enjoy panoramic valley views, and experience thrilling rides at Scenic World, including the Scenic Railway, Skyway, and Cableway.
Overnight stay in Sydney.

Day 10 | Departure

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and enjoy a private transfer to the airport for your return flight.
Depart with unforgettable memories of Australia’s iconic cities, wildlife, coastlines, and adventures.
