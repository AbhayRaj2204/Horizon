Australia City Escape – Melbourne & Sydney

Melbourne & Sydney | 9 Days / 8 Nights

Tour Itinerary

Day 01 | Arrive in Melbourne – Leisure Day

Welcome to Melbourne, Australia’s cultural capital! On arrival, you will be greeted by our representative and transferred privately to your hotel. Check in and unwind after your journey.
The remainder of the day is free to explore at your own pace. Discover Melbourne’s lively city centre, stroll around Federation Square, or enjoy a relaxed walk along the Yarra River precinct.
Overnight stay in Melbourne.

Day 02 | Melbourne City Tour & Phillip Island Penguin Parade (SIC)

After breakfast, set out on a half-day Melbourne City Tour, covering key landmarks such as St. Patrick’s Cathedral, Fitzroy Gardens, and the historic Royal Exhibition Building.
In the afternoon, travel to Phillip Island for the famous Penguin Parade. As the sun sets, watch the adorable little penguins waddle ashore after a day at sea—an unforgettable wildlife experience.
Overnight stay in Melbourne.

Day 03 | Great Ocean Road & 12 Apostles Tour (SIC)

Enjoy breakfast before departing on a full-day Great Ocean Road Tour, one of the world’s most scenic coastal drives.
Marvel at dramatic ocean views, pristine beaches, and iconic natural formations, including the breathtaking 12 Apostles limestone stacks. Return to Melbourne in the evening.
Overnight stay in Melbourne.

Day 04 | Day at Leisure or Optional Excursions

After breakfast, enjoy a relaxed day at leisure. Explore Melbourne’s famous laneway cafés, shop at Queen Victoria Market, or simply soak in the city’s vibrant atmosphere.
Optional Tours (at extra cost):
• Yarra Valley Wine Tour
• Puffing Billy Steam Train & Dandenong Ranges
Overnight stay in Melbourne.

Day 05 | Fly to Sydney – Leisure Evening

After breakfast, check out and transfer to the airport for your flight to Sydney.
On arrival, enjoy a private transfer to your hotel and check in. Spend the evening at leisure—take a stroll around Darling Harbour or enjoy waterfront dining.
Overnight stay in Sydney.

Day 06 | Sydney City Tour & Opera House Guided Visit

After breakfast, explore Sydney using the Big Bus Hop-On Hop-Off Pass. Visit iconic attractions such as The Rocks, Hyde Park, Parliament House, Mrs Macquarie’s Chair, and Bondi Beach.
Later, enjoy an inside guided tour of the Sydney Opera House, one of the world’s most recognizable landmarks. Evening free to explore Circular Quay.
Overnight stay in Sydney.

Day 07 | Blue Mountains & Scenic World Tour (SIC)

After breakfast, depart for a full-day Blue Mountains excursion.
Visit Echo Point for views of the famous Three Sisters, experience thrilling rides at Scenic World including the Scenic Railway, Cableway, and Skyway, and enjoy close encounters with native wildlife at Featherdale Wildlife Park.
Return to Sydney by evening.
Overnight stay in Sydney.

Day 08 | Leisure Day & Sydney Harbour Dinner Cruise

Morning at leisure or explore optional attractions such as Sydney Tower Eye or SEA LIFE Aquarium.
In the evening, enjoy a memorable Sydney Harbour Dinner Cruise with private transfers. Relish a delicious buffet dinner, live entertainment, and stunning night views of Sydney’s illuminated skyline.
Overnight stay in Sydney.

Day 09 | Departure

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to the airport for your onward flight.
Return home with unforgettable memories of Australia’s iconic cities, scenic landscapes, and unique wildlife.
