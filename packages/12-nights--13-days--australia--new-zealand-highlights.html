12 Nights / 13 Days – Australia & New Zealand Highlights

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Sydney | Leisure

On arrival in Sydney, you will be transferred to your centrally located hotel via a shared coach service. After check-in, the remainder of the day is free at leisure.
Sydney, the capital of New South Wales, is one of Australia’s most iconic cities, known for its stunning harbour, the Sydney Opera House, Harbour Bridge, Darling Harbour and lush Royal Botanic Gardens. You may choose to relax after your journey or explore the city at your own pace.

Day 2: Sydney | City Sightseeing & Bondi Beach

After breakfast, join your coach from the designated pick-up point for an afternoon Sydney city sightseeing tour combined with a visit to Bondi Beach.
Travel through the historic Rocks precinct and enjoy views of major landmarks including the Sydney Opera House, Hyde Park, St Mary’s Cathedral, Parliament House and the Art Gallery of New South Wales. Stop at Mrs Macquarie’s Chair for excellent photo opportunities of the Opera House and Harbour Bridge.
Continue to The Gap for dramatic coastal views before heading to the world-famous Bondi Beach. Return to the city via Paddington and Sydney’s exclusive Eastern Suburbs.

Day 3: Sydney | Blue Mountains & Australian Wildlife Experience

After breakfast, depart for a full-day excursion to the World Heritage-listed Blue Mountains National Park. Discover dramatic cliffs, deep valleys, waterfalls and native bushland stretching over one million hectares.
Visit Echo Point for close-up views of the iconic Three Sisters rock formation, followed by time at Scenic World, where optional rides such as the Scenic Railway, Cableway or Skyway are available (at own expense).
Enjoy free time for lunch in the charming village of Leura before continuing to Sydney Zoo. Here, experience an Australian Bush Safari with close encounters with native wildlife including kangaroos, koalas, wombats and dingoes, along with an Aboriginal-guided cultural experience.

Day 4: Sydney – Gold Coast | Leisure

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and transfer to Sydney Airport for your flight to the Gold Coast.
On arrival, transfer to your centrally located hotel via shared coach service. The remainder of the day is free to relax or explore the Gold Coast independently, known for its beaches, dining scene and relaxed coastal atmosphere.

Day 5: Gold Coast | Sea World

After breakfast, enjoy a full day at Sea World, one of Australia’s most popular marine theme parks.
Experience a mix of marine attractions, shows and rides suitable for all ages. Highlights include Shark Bay, one of the world’s largest man-made lagoon systems for sharks, and the engaging Planet SOS 4-D presentation, combining entertainment with environmental awareness.

Day 6: Gold Coast | Warner Bros. Movie World

After breakfast, visit Warner Bros. Movie World, Australia’s only movie-themed amusement park.
Enjoy a day filled with thrilling rides, live shows and behind-the-scenes movie magic. Highlights include adrenaline-packed attractions such as Superman Escape and Batwing Spaceshot, along with family-friendly entertainment and character experiences.

Day 7: Gold Coast – Queenstown | Leisure

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and transfer to Brisbane Airport for your flight to Queenstown.
On arrival, transfer to your hotel via shared coach service. After check-in, the remainder of the day is at leisure.
Queenstown is a vibrant alpine town offering excellent dining, cafés and nightlife. A list of recommended eateries and optional activities will be shared closer to arrival.

Day 8: Queenstown | Milford Sound Cruise

After breakfast, depart on a full-day Milford Sound tour. Cruise through one of the world’s most spectacular natural landscapes, surrounded by towering cliffs, waterfalls and pristine waters.
See highlights such as Bowen Falls and Stirling Falls, and keep an eye out for wildlife including fur seals, dolphins, penguins and, on occasion, whales. Some vessels also feature underwater viewing areas.

Day 9: Queenstown | Skyline Gondola (Self-Transfer)

After breakfast, make your way independently to enjoy a ride on the Skyline Queenstown Gondola.
Ascend Bob’s Peak aboard the Southern Hemisphere’s steepest gondola and take in panoramic views over Queenstown, Lake Wakatipu and the surrounding mountain ranges.

Day 10: Queenstown | Thunder Jet Boat Ride (25 Minutes)

After breakfast, experience an exhilarating 25-minute Thunder Jet Boat ride departing from Queenstown town centre.
Enjoy high-speed thrills, sharp turns and signature 360-degree spins as you skim across Lake Wakatipu, passing close to the shoreline and travelling under two bridges at speed.

Day 11: Queenstown – Franz Josef | Glacier Hot Pools

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and transfer to Franz Josef.
In the evening, relax at the Glacier Hot Pools, set within lush rainforest surroundings. These naturally heated pools offer the perfect opportunity to unwind after your West Coast journey, providing a peaceful and rejuvenating experience.

Day 12: Franz Josef – Christchurch | TranzAlpine Train

After breakfast, check out and transfer to Greymouth Railway Station to board the renowned TranzAlpine Train to Christchurch.
Often regarded as one of the world’s great scenic rail journeys, the route passes through alpine landscapes, river valleys and dramatic mountain scenery.
On arrival in Christchurch, transfer to your hotel. The evening is free at leisure.

Day 13: Departure | Christchurch

As per your flight schedule, transfer to Christchurch Airport via shared coach service for your onward journey, returning home with wonderful memories of Australia and New Zealand.
