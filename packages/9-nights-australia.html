Ready to Plan Your Dream Journey?

9 Nights / 10 Days Australia Holiday Package

Melbourne • Gold Coast • Sydney

Tour Itinerary

Day 01 – Arrival in Melbourne

Arrive at Melbourne Tullamarine Airport and enjoy a private transfer to your centrally located CBD hotel.Check in after 3:00 PM and spend the rest of the day at leisure exploring Melbourne’s famous laneways, cafés, and riverfront.
Overnight: Mercure Melbourne Southbank or similar

Day 02 – Phillip Island Penguin Parade & Wildlife Tour

After breakfast, proceed on a Small Group Eco Tour to Phillip Island:
Photo stop at the iconic Brighton Beach Bathing Boxes
Guided visit to Moonlit Sanctuary Wildlife Conservation Park
Meet kangaroos, wallabies, emus, koalas, wombats & more
Visit The Nobbies & Seal Rocks for spectacular coastal views
Walk scenic boardwalks along the rugged coastline
Witness the world-famous Penguin Parade at dusk (standard seating)
Overnight: Mercure Melbourne Southbank or similar

Day 03 – Great Ocean Road Full-Day Tour

Experience one of the world’s most scenic coastal drives:
Complimentary morning tea along the Surf Coast
Photo stops along the Great Ocean Road
Spot wild koalas at Kennett River
Lunch break (at own expense) in Apollo Bay
Guided rainforest walk in the Otway National Park
Visit the Twelve Apostles, Loch Ard Gorge, Razorback & Island Arch
Walk down Gibson Steps for dramatic ocean views
Overnight: Mercure Melbourne Southbank or similar

Day 04 – Melbourne Departure

After breakfast, check out and transfer privately to Melbourne Airport for your onward flight.
Gold Coast | 3 Nights

Day 04 – Arrival on the Gold Coast

Arrive at Brisbane Airport and enjoy a private transfer to your Gold Coast hotel.Check in after 3:00 PM and relax by the beach or explore the lively surroundings.
Overnight: Vibe Hotel Gold Coast or similar

Day 05 – Warner Bros. Movie World

Enjoy a thrilling day at Movie World:
SIC return transfers
Full-day admission ticket
Access to movie-themed rides, shows, and attractions
Overnight: Vibe Hotel Gold Coast or similar

Day 06 – Sea World Adventure

Spend the day at Sea World, perfect for families and thrill-seekers:
SIC return transfers
Full-day admission ticket
Marine shows, animal encounters, and exciting rides
Overnight: Vibe Hotel Gold Coast or similar

Day 07 – Gold Coast Departure

After breakfast, check out and transfer privately to Brisbane Airport for your flight to Sydney.
Sydney | 3 Nights

Day 07 – Arrival in Sydney

Arrive at Sydney Airport and enjoy a private transfer to your CBD hotel.Check in after 3:00 PM and enjoy the evening at leisure.
Overnight: Holiday Inn Potts Point Sydney or similar

Day 08 – Sydney City Tour & Opera House

Enjoy a Panoramic Sydney City Tour, covering:
Kings Cross & Double Bay
Watsons Bay
Bondi Beach (30-minute stop)
Tamarama & Bronte Beaches
Centennial Park & Paddington
Photo stop at Mrs Macquarie’s Point
Historic Rocks Area & CBD
Final stop at Darling Harbour
Later, enjoy:
Sydney Opera House 1-Hour Guided Tour (English)
2-Attraction Pass (choose any two):
Sydney Zoo, SEA LIFE Sydney Aquarium, Madame Tussauds, Sydney Tower Eye
Overnight: Holiday Inn Potts Point Sydney or similar

Day 09 – Blue Mountains Experience

Explore the natural beauty of the Blue Mountains:
Visit Sydney Zoo with kangaroo feeding & koala photo opportunity
Explore Leura Village
Stop at Echo Point to view the Three Sisters
Enjoy unlimited rides at Scenic World
Evening return to Sydney CBD
Overnight: Holiday Inn Potts Point Sydney or similar

Day 10 – Departure from Sydney

After breakfast, check out and enjoy a private transfer to Sydney Airport.Depart with unforgettable memories of Australia.
