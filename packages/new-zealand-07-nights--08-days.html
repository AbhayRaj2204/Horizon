Tour Itinerary

Day 01: Arrival in Auckland

On arrival at Auckland Airport, you will be met and privately transferred to your hotel. After check-in, the remainder of the day is free at leisure to relax or explore at your own pace.
Overnight stay in Auckland.

Day 02: Auckland City Tour & Sky Tower

Today, enjoy a guided half-day city tour showcasing the highlights of New Zealand’s largest city. Travel in a small group with an experienced guide, taking in views of Auckland Harbour, crossing the iconic Harbour Bridge, and enjoying panoramic vistas from Mt Eden. The tour also includes scenic drives past Mission Bay beaches, with plenty of photo opportunities along the way.
Later, visit the Auckland Sky Tower independently and take in sweeping views over the city and surrounding harbours.
Included: Auckland City Tour & Sky Tower entry.
Overnight stay in Auckland.

Day 03: Waitomo Glowworm Caves – Rotorua

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and proceed for the Waitomo Glowworm Caves tour. Travel through the rolling countryside before exploring this remarkable underground cave system, formed over millions of years.
Walk through the caverns before boarding a boat for a tranquil glide through the Glowworm Grotto, where thousands of glowworms illuminate the darkness. Unique to New Zealand, this natural display is a truly memorable experience.
After the tour, continue on to Rotorua and check in to your hotel.
Included: Waitomo Glowworm Caves tour.
Overnight stay in Rotorua.

Day 04: Rotorua | Geothermal & Cultural Experiences

Rotorua is renowned for its dramatic geothermal landscapes, featuring steaming vents, erupting geysers and bubbling mud pools, set against native forests and pristine lakes. The region is also deeply connected to Māori culture and heritage.
Today, visit Waimangu Geothermal Valley, followed by an evening experience at Redwoods Nightlights, where illuminated forest walkways create a unique and immersive atmosphere.
Included: Waimangu Geothermal Valley & Redwoods Nightlights.
Overnight stay in Rotorua.

Day 05: Rotorua – Queenstown

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and transfer to Rotorua Airport for your flight to Queenstown.
On arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel and assisted with check-in. The remainder of the day is at leisure.
Overnight stay in Queenstown.

Day 06: Milford Sound Tour

Today, experience one of New Zealand’s most iconic natural wonders. Travel through Fiordland National Park, passing mirror lakes, waterfalls and glacier-carved landscapes before reaching Milford Sound, often referred to as the “Eighth Wonder of the World”.
Enjoy a scenic cruise through the fiord, surrounded by towering cliffs and cascading waterfalls, before returning to Queenstown in the evening.
Duration: Approximately 12 hours.
Included: Milford Sound tour.
Overnight stay in Queenstown.

Day 07: Queenstown | Gondola & Luge Experience

Begin the day with a scenic Skyline Gondola ride, ascending 480 metres above Lake Wakatipu to the top of Bob’s Peak. Take in panoramic views of Queenstown, Coronet Peak, The Remarkables and the surrounding alpine scenery.
Follow this with an exciting Luge experience, navigating a 1,600-metre track of curves, tunnels and dips. After each run, return via the chairlift and enjoy another ride.
Included: Skyline Gondola with 3 Luge rides.
Overnight stay in Queenstown.

Day 08: Departure from Queenstown

Today, your arrangements conclude with a scheduled transfer to Queenstown Airport for your onward journey.
