8 Days Ultimate Australian Adventure

4 Nights Gold Coast | 3 Nights Cairns

Tour Itinerary

Day 01 | Arrival in Gold Coast – Evening City Lights Dinner Cruise

Welcome to the vibrant Gold Coast! Upon arrival at Brisbane Airport, meet our representative and enjoy a comfortable private transfer to your hotel. Check in and unwind after your journey.
As evening falls, set sail on a Surfers Paradise City Lights Sightseeing Cruise. Glide through sparkling waterways while admiring the illuminated skyline of Surfers Paradise. Capture stunning photographs and relax onboard while enjoying cheese and crackers, creating the perfect start to your Australian adventure.
Overnight Stay: Gold Coast

Day 02 | Sunrise Hot Air Ballooning & Theme Park Thrills

Start your day before sunrise with a truly magical experience — a Hot Air Balloon Flight over the scenic Gold Coast hinterland. Float gently above rolling landscapes as the sun rises, followed by a light breakfast.
Later, enjoy a full-day visit to one iconic Gold Coast theme park (any one):
• Dreamworld or
• Warner Bros. Movie World
Experience thrilling rides, live entertainment, and world-class attractions that promise excitement for all ages.
Overnight Stay: Gold Coast

Day 03 | Jet Boat Adventure, Helicopter Flight & Sea World

After breakfast, prepare for an adrenaline-charged day along the coast.
Experience:
• Jet Boat Ride – high-speed spins and heart-pounding thrills across Surfers Paradise and Broadbeach
• Helicopter Scenic Flight – spectacular aerial views of the coastline, beaches, and city skyline
Continue the adventure with a visit to Sea World, where marine life encounters, exciting shows, and family attractions await.
Evening free to explore Surfers Paradise at your own pace.
Overnight Stay: Gold Coast

Day 04 | Springbrook National Park – Natural Bridge & Waterfalls Tour (5 Hours)

Today, escape into nature with a guided tour to Springbrook National Park, one of Queensland’s most beautiful rainforest regions.
Tour highlights include:
• Guided rainforest walk to the stunning Natural Bridge Waterfall
• Panoramic views from the famous “Best of All Lookout”
• Sightseeing of up to five waterfalls, including Purling Brook Falls
• Scenic drive through the picturesque Numinbah Valley, known for its dramatic cliffs and lush green pastures
• Light refreshments during the tour
Return to Gold Coast by afternoon.
Overnight Stay: Gold Coast

Day 05 | Gold Coast – Cairns

After breakfast, check out and transfer to the airport for your flight to Cairns, the gateway to the Great Barrier Reef.
On arrival, transfer to your hotel and check in. The rest of the day is free to explore the Cairns Esplanade, waterfront promenades, or vibrant local markets.
Overnight Stay: Cairns

Day 06 | Great Barrier Reef Cruise with Scuba Diving & BBQ Lunch

Today is the highlight of your journey — a full-day excursion to the Great Barrier Reef, one of the world’s greatest natural wonders.
Outer Reef Cruise inclusions:
• Complimentary return bus transfers from Cairns CBD
• Morning tea or coffee
• Hot breakfast with bacon & egg rolls
• Educational presentation by a Marine Biologist
• Free scuba dive (introductory or certified)
• Snorkelling at pristine reef locations
• Freshly prepared BBQ lunch with meats, vegetables, and salads
• Unlimited snorkelling at a second reef site
• Complimentary glass of wine with cheese & crackers
A truly unforgettable marine experience.
Overnight Stay: Cairns

Day 07 | Kuranda Rainforest Experience

After breakfast, embark on the iconic Kuranda Rainforest Tour, showcasing the best of tropical North Queensland.
Experience includes:
• Skyrail Rainforest Cableway with panoramic views over the rainforest canopy
• Free time at Kuranda Village for shopping and exploration
• Scenic Railway Journey, winding through lush rainforest, waterfalls, and tunnels
Return to Cairns by evening.
Overnight Stay: Cairns

Day 08 | Departure from Cairns

After breakfast, check out and transfer to Cairns Airport for your onward journey, carrying home unforgettable memories of Australia’s coastlines, rainforests, and reef adventures.

  • Destinations: Theme Parks | Coastal Thrills | Rainforest Wonders | Great Barrier Reef
