New Zealand 10 Nights / 11 Days

Package 2

Tour Itinerary

Day 01: Arrival in Auckland

Arrive at Auckland Airport and enjoy a private transfer to your hotel. After check-in, the remainder of the day is free at leisure to relax or explore at your own pace.
Overnight stay in Auckland.

Day 02: Auckland City Tour & Sky Tower

This morning, enjoy a half-day guided city tour showcasing the highlights of New Zealand’s largest city. Travel in a small group with an experienced guide as you take in views of Auckland Harbour, cross the iconic Harbour Bridge, and enjoy panoramic city vistas from Mt Eden. The tour also includes scenic drives past Mission Bay, with plenty of photo opportunities along the way.
Later, visit the Auckland Sky Tower at your own pace and enjoy sweeping views across the city and surrounding harbours.
Included: Auckland City Tour & Sky Tower entry
Overnight stay in Auckland.

Day 03: Waitomo Glowworm Caves | Rotorua

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and proceed for the Waitomo Glowworm Caves experience. Travel through the lush green countryside of Waitomo before descending into an underground world shaped over millions of years.
Explore the caves on foot before boarding a boat for a peaceful journey through the Glowworm Grotto, illuminated by thousands of glowworms unique to New Zealand. The calm atmosphere and natural light display make this a truly memorable experience.
Following the tour, continue on to Rotorua and check in to your hotel.
Included: Waitomo Glowworm Caves Tour
Overnight stay in Rotorua.

Day 04: Rotorua | Geothermal & Forest Experience

Rotorua is renowned for its dramatic geothermal landscapes, featuring steaming vents, erupting geysers and bubbling mud pools, all set among lakes and native forests. The region is also rich in Māori culture, legends and traditions.
Today, visit Waimangu Geothermal Valley to explore one of Rotorua’s most impressive natural attractions, followed by an evening visit to Redwoods Nightlights, a unique sensory experience set among towering forest trees.
Included: Waimangu Geothermal Valley & Redwoods Nightlights
Overnight stay in Rotorua.

Day 05: Rotorua – Christchurch

Today, transfer to the airport for your domestic flight to Christchurch (airfare not included). The flight duration is approximately two hours.
On arrival, discover Christchurch, a city beautifully positioned between the Pacific Ocean and the snow-capped Southern Alps. Known for its gardens, river walks and creative energy, Christchurch blends natural beauty with a modern, evolving cityscape.
Overnight stay in Christchurch.

Day 06: TranzAlpine Train | Franz Josef Glacier

This morning, transfer to Christchurch Railway Station to board the iconic TranzAlpine Train. Journey across the Canterbury Plains, through the spectacular Waimakariri River gorge and into the heart of the Southern Alps at Arthur’s Pass National Park.
On arrival in Greymouth, meet your driver and continue by road to the Franz Josef Glacier region.
Included: TranzAlpine Train tickets
Overnight stay in Franz Josef.

Day 07: Franz Josef Glacier Region

After breakfast, enjoy a 20-minute scenic helicopter flight, offering breathtaking aerial views of glaciers, alpine peaks and rugged landscapes.
The remainder of the day is free to relax or explore the Franz Josef Glacier region at your leisure.
Included: 20-minute Scenic Helicopter Ride
Overnight stay in Franz Josef.

Day 08: Franz Josef – Queenstown

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and travel by coach to Queenstown, passing through some of New Zealand’s most scenic landscapes.
On arrival, check in to your hotel and enjoy the rest of the day at leisure.
Overnight stay in Queenstown.

Day 09: Milford Sound Tour

Today, experience one of the world’s last great wilderness areas with a full-day tour to Milford Sound. Travel past mirror lakes and cascading waterfalls into the heart of Fiordland National Park, often described as the eighth wonder of the world.
Board a cruise and glide through the fiord, surrounded by towering cliffs and dramatic mountain scenery. Get close to waterfalls and keep an eye out for local wildlife. Lunch is included.
After a memorable day, return to Queenstown by coach.
Overnight stay in Queenstown.

Day 10: Queenstown | Gondola & Luge

Begin the day with a ride on the Skyline Gondola, ascending 480 metres above Lake Wakatipu to Bob’s Peak for panoramic views of Queenstown and the surrounding mountain ranges.
Enjoy three exciting Luge rides, navigating a fun and scenic downhill track suitable for all ages.
Included: Skyline Gondola & 3 Luge Rides
Overnight stay in Queenstown.

Day 11: Departure | Queenstown

Your arrangements conclude today with a transfer to Queenstown Airport for your onward journey.
