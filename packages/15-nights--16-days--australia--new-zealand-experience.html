15 Nights / 16 Days – Australia & New Zealand Experience

15 Nights / 16 Days – Australia & New Zealand Experience

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Sydney | Leisure

Welcome to Sydney. On arrival, you will be transferred to your centrally located hotel via shared coach. After check-in, the remainder of the day is free to relax or explore at your own pace.
Sydney, the capital of New South Wales, is renowned for its stunning harbour, iconic Sydney Opera House, Harbour Bridge, Darling Harbour and beautiful waterfront precincts. Guests may also choose to enjoy panoramic city views from the Sydney Tower Skywalk.

Day 2: Sydney City Highlights & Bondi Beach

After breakfast, join your coach from the designated pickup point for an afternoon city sightseeing tour combined with Bondi Beach.
The tour takes you through the historic Rocks precinct, past key landmarks including the Opera House, Hyde Park, St Mary’s Cathedral, Parliament House and the Art Gallery of NSW. Enjoy photo stops at Mrs Macquarie’s Chair for spectacular harbour views and The Gap, known for its dramatic coastal scenery.
The tour concludes with a visit to the world-famous Bondi Beach, before returning to the city via Paddington and Sydney’s exclusive Eastern Suburbs.

Day 3: Blue Mountains Tour with Australian Wildlife

After breakfast, depart for a full-day excursion to the World Heritage–listed Blue Mountains National Park. Discover dramatic landscapes of sandstone cliffs, deep valleys and eucalyptus forests.
Visit Echo Point for close-up views of the iconic Three Sisters, followed by time at Scenic World, where optional rides such as the Scenic Railway, Skyway or Cableway are available (at own expense).
Later, enjoy free time in the charming village of Leura, ideal for lunch and a stroll through boutique shops. The day concludes with a visit to Sydney Zoo, where you’ll encounter native Australian wildlife including kangaroos, koalas, wombats and emus in open, cage-free environments, along with an Aboriginal-led cultural experience.

Day 4: Sydney – Cairns | Leisure

After breakfast, check out from your Sydney hotel and transfer to the airport for your flight to Cairns.
On arrival, enjoy a shared transfer to your hotel in the city centre. The rest of the day is free to relax and enjoy the tropical atmosphere of Far North Queensland.

Day 5: Great Barrier Reef | Green Island Tour with Lunch

After breakfast, depart for a full-day Green Island experience. Travel aboard a modern, air-conditioned catamaran to this beautiful coral cay on the Great Barrier Reef.
Spend approximately four hours enjoying the island’s pristine beaches, coral reefs and rainforest walking tracks. Included activities feature a semi-submarine tour, glass-bottom boat experience or snorkelling equipment. Lunch is served onboard the vessel.
Optional activities such as scuba diving, helmet diving or beach equipment hire are available at an additional cost.

Day 6: Cairns – Gold Coast | Leisure

After breakfast, check out and transfer to the airport for your flight to the Gold Coast.
On arrival, transfer to your hotel in the city centre. The evening is free to explore the Gold Coast’s vibrant dining scene or simply relax after your journey.

Day 7: Gold Coast | Sea World

After breakfast, enjoy a full day at Sea World, one of Australia’s most popular marine theme parks.
Experience a mix of marine life encounters, entertaining shows and family-friendly rides. Highlights include Shark Bay, one of the world’s largest man-made shark lagoons, and immersive attractions such as Planet SOS, a 4D presentation designed to educate through entertainment.

Day 8: Gold Coast | Warner Bros. Movie World

Today, visit Warner Bros. Movie World, where Hollywood comes to life.
Enjoy action-packed rides, live shows, stunt performances and themed attractions. Thrill-seekers can experience highlights such as Superman Escape and Batwing Spaceshot, while families can enjoy character meet-and-greets and entertainment throughout the park.

Day 9: Gold Coast – Queenstown | Leisure

After breakfast, check out and transfer to Brisbane Airport for your flight to Queenstown.
On arrival, enjoy a shared transfer to your hotel. The rest of the day is at leisure to explore Queenstown’s lively waterfront, cafés and restaurants. A list of recommended dining options will be provided closer to arrival, and optional activities can be arranged on request.

Day 10: Milford Sound Cruise (Ex Queenstown)

After breakfast, depart on a full-day tour to Milford Sound, one of New Zealand’s most iconic natural attractions.
Cruise through dramatic fiords, towering cliffs and cascading waterfalls such as Bowen Falls and Stirling Falls. Keep an eye out for wildlife including fur seals, dolphins, penguins and, on occasion, whales. Some vessels offer underwater viewing observatories for a unique perspective.

Day 11: Queenstown | Skyline Gondola (Self Transfer)

After breakfast, make your way independently to experience the Skyline Gondola.
Ride the Southern Hemisphere’s steepest gondola to the top of Bob’s Peak, enjoying spectacular panoramic views over Queenstown, Lake Wakatipu and the surrounding alpine scenery.

Day 12: Queenstown – Christchurch | Scenic Transfer

After breakfast, check out and commence a full-day journey to Christchurch.
Travel through the Lindis Pass, into the Mackenzie Country and through Aoraki / Mount Cook National Park, home to New Zealand’s highest peak. Enjoy free time for lunch in Mount Cook Village (own expense) before continuing past Lake Pukaki, Lake Tekapo and the iconic Church of the Good Shepherd.
Continue across the Canterbury Plains before arriving in Christchurch.

Day 13: Christchurch – Auckland | Sky Tower Experience

After breakfast, check out and transfer to Christchurch Airport for your flight to Auckland.
On arrival in the “City of Sails”, transfer to your hotel. The remainder of the day is free.
In the evening, visit the iconic Sky Tower, offering exceptional views, dining options and adventure experiences.

Day 14: Auckland City Tour

After breakfast, enjoy a half-day guided tour of Auckland.
Travel through the city centre, Ponsonby, across the Auckland Harbour Bridge, and along the waterfront at Viaduct Harbour and Queen Street. Visit the Auckland Domain, home to the Auckland War Memorial Museum, before exploring the historic village of Parnell.
The tour continues along Tamaki Drive to Michael Joseph Savage Memorial Park, offering panoramic views over Auckland’s eastern bays and harbour.

Day 15: Auckland – Waitomo Glowworm Caves

After breakfast, depart on a full-day tour to the Waitomo Glowworm Caves.
Travel through the scenic Waikato region before enjoying a guided cave tour, learning about ancient limestone formations. Conclude with a magical silent boat ride through the Glowworm Grotto, illuminated by thousands of glowworms.
Enjoy free time at the visitor complex before returning to Auckland.

Day 16: Departure

As per your flight schedule, enjoy a shared transfer to Auckland Airport for your onward journey, departing with wonderful memories of Australia and New Zealand.
